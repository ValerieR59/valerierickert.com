Valerie Rickert, CCP

Valerie is an experienced full stack developer, specializing in database applications. A certified computing professional, Valerie received her Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Houston.

Currently, she is sharpening her skillset in HTML, CSS, and Javascript by building and deploying web and mobile applications through Meteor and Mongo DB. See her varied skillset below.

Valerie's goal is to contribute to the OpenSource Community through application development for Not-For-Profit corporations and participation in local Hackathons.

She would also like to encourage young women in her community to join her in the industry. At most, women today make up only 25% of the tech workforce. By mentoring budding women-in-tech, Valerie hopes to help close the gap!
